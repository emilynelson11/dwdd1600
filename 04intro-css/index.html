Color Palettes

Choosing a color palette sets the tone for the entire wedding. From attire to décor, your colors tie everything together and help create the atmosphere you want for your big day. Here are some of the most popular wedding color palettes to inspire your planning.

Trending Colors

Pastel Rainbows (Blush, Lavender, Butter Yellow, Mint, Baby Blue)
Dreamy and soft, ideal for spring celebrations.
Blush & Sage
Romantic, timeless, and chic—especially lovely with eucalyptus and white florals.
Black & White
A timeless and elegant palette that’s all about classic sophistication. Crisp white paired with deep black creates a clean, modern look that works beautifully for formal or minimalist weddings, letting shapes, textures, and metallic accents shine.
Shades of Green
A fresh, natural palette featuring everything from soft sage to deep forest green. This versatile combination evokes calm, elegance, and a connection to nature—perfect for outdoor, garden, or botanical-inspired weddings.
Shades of Blue
A serene and elegant palette ranging from soft powder blue to deep navy. Blue evokes calm, romance, and timeless sophistication, making it perfect for seaside, winter, or classic-themed weddings.
