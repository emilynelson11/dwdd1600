Who Were the Signers

Famous Signers

Some of the most famous signers of the Declaration of Independence include:

Thomas Jefferson
Principal author of the Declaration of Independence.
John Adams
A strong advocate for independence and future 2nd U.S. president.
Benjamin Franklin
Diplomat, inventor, and a leading figure in early American politics.
John Hancock
President of the Continental Congress, famous for his large signature.
Samuel Adams
Key organizer of resistance against British policies.

The Declaration of Independence was shaped and signed by many influential figures who played crucial roles in America's fight for freedom. Leaders like Thomas Jefferson, John Adams, Benjamin Franklin, John Hancock, and Samuel Adams helped define the nation's ideals and set the foundation for its future. Their courage and leadership continue to be remembered as central to the birth of the United States.
